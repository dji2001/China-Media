China Foreign Coverage Explorer

This website provides an interactive interface to explore the data from the paper: “Domestic Politics and Editorial Control Over Foreign News Coverage in the People’s Daily, 1993–2022”
by Jianbing Li, Jiakun Jack Zhang, Duoji Jiang, and Weifeng Zhong
Journal Version
Open-source Preprint

About the Project

This project investigates how domestic political dynamics in China—especially power consolidation among top leaders—affect foreign coverage in the country’s most important state newspaper, the People’s Daily. Surprisingly, as China's global presence expands, the amount of foreign coverage in its flagship paper has decreased significantly under Xi Jinping’s administration.

To support this research, we developed the CFC Database, which records the number of articles referencing specific countries (defined as having the country keyword appear more than twice), their proportion relative to the total number of articles each year, and supplementary data such as trade volumes and leadership visits. This dataset enables users to explore trends in the frequency of different countries’ appearances in Chinese state media.

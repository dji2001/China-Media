China Foreign Coverage Explorer

This website provides an interactive interface to explore the findings from the paper: “Domestic Politics and Editorial Control Over Foreign News Coverage in the People’s Daily, 1993–2022”
by Jianbing Li, Jiakun Jack Zhang, Duoji Jiang, and Weifeng Zhong
Read the paper

About the Project

This project investigates how domestic political dynamics in China—especially power consolidation among top leaders—affect foreign coverage in the country’s most important state newspaper, the People’s Daily. Surprisingly, as China's global presence expands, the amount of foreign coverage in its flagship paper has decreased significantly under Xi Jinping’s administration.

To support this research, we constructed the CFC Database, which includes over 1 million articles published between 1993 and 2022. Articles are tagged with country mentions, and the dataset allows users to track trends in how often different countries appear in Chinese state media.
